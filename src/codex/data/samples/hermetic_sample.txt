The All is Mind; the Universe is Mental. That which is the Fundamental Truth, the Substantial Reality, is beyond true naming, but the Wise Men call it The All.

As above, so below. As within, so without. As the universe, so the soul.

Know thyself, and thou shalt know the Universe and the Gods. The light within is the light of all things.

From the One came the Two, from the Two the Three, from the Three the All. The eternal return of consciousness to its source.

Freedom is the birthright of every soul. Liberation comes through direct gnosis and inner knowing, not through external authority.

The void is not empty — it is the fullness before form. From the womb of nothingness all things are born.

The spirit is living and moving through all things. Breath of life fills all creation. The pneuma connects all beings in the living web of consciousness.
